/.About Us

Growth Tech. Solutions (Pty) LTD is a construction company that focuses on Architecture, General Building & Construction Consultation. It is led by young and motivated individuals who are professionally trained in the field of Civil Engineering. We have access to the latest and growing technology that is available in construction today.

We strive in the utilization of such various Design and Performance tools, Analytic software, Drafting, CAD and Building Information Modelling (BIM) to provide the analysis and using REVIT Drawing software allows for the ease of Architectural Designs and co-ordination to minimize clashes on site hence improve efficiency and quality of our services.

Architecture

As-Built Layout Drawings

Accurate as-built drawings for System Maintenance. Renovations & Project Planning Projects.

Architectural Designs

To create a project that our clients desire. Our designs takes into account the ideas of the client so to make a creative & aesthetic modern architectural design.

Proposal Submissions

Pre-Planning is one of the crucial phases of any construction project and having to comply with the local municipality regulations is of utmost important. We have Pr. Architects that are registered under SACAP for proposal submissions to the Municipality.

Civil Engineering & Construction

Bill of Quantities (quantity surveying)

Accurate project costing to provides a clear and detailed record of the estimated costs for the project, which can be used to Tender, Budget, Monitor and Control Spending.

Condition Inspections

Experienced technicians to provide accurate assessment of buildings/structures that require repairs or maintenance.

Construction Management/Supervision

Managing Contractors or Subcontractors to ensure quality and project completion within the required specification, scope and time.

Engineering Structural Designs

Structural Engineering designs of structural elements such as retaining walls, foundation, columns, beams etc.

Land Surveying

As part of the planning phase of a construction project, it is necessary to carry out surveying work. We provide essential surveying services such as beacon relocation and topographic surveys.
